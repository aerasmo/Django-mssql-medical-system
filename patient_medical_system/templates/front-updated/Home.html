Eye Care

Here are the types of services that our hospital offer

Diagnosis and tests

Diagnose eye conditions to start treatment and to gather general idea what is causing discomfort. You can also check your eye health to have an idea what is going on for preventive maintenance.

Prescriptions and therapeutics

Fit eyeglass lenses and frames and contact lenses to correct vision problems. Provide medicine and therapy to relieve pain

Surgery

We can also provide surgeries for eye injuries and diseases. LASIK is the most commonly performed laser refractive surgery to treat corneal myopia (nearsightedness), hyperopia (farsightedness), and astigmatism.

Eye health impacts overall wellness

Eyes are an essential part of our lives and we believe it should be well-taken care of. Our team can care for everyone in the family, from toddlers to seniors. And because we have an extensive team of eye specialists, we can help you with all of your eye needs.
